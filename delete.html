Delete Your JustCooked Account

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the JustCooked app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your profile information (name, email)
  • Your saved recipes
  • Your pantry items
  • Your cooking history
  • Your preferences and settings

Data Retention

  • Account data is deleted immediately upon request
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained for up to 90 days
  • Backup copies are purged within 30 days
