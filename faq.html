Health & Dietary

Our system utilizes comprehensive hidden allergen detection. For example, if you are allergic to peanuts, it won't just avoid "peanuts"—it will also exclude peanut oil, satay sauce, and dishes commonly associated with peanut ingredients. You can customize your allergy profile in the app settings.
We support over 12 dietary patterns, including Mediterranean, Keto, Paleo, Vegan, Vegetarian, Pescatarian, Halal, and Kosher. You can combine multiple preferences to suit your lifestyle.
