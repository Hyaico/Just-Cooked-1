How Can We Help?

Before You Contact Us

To help us resolve your issue as quickly as possible, please have the following information ready:

  • The email address associated with your Just Cooked account
  • A description of the issue you're experiencing
  • Your device type (iPhone, Android) and operating system version
  • The version of the Just Cooked app you're using
  • Any error messages you've received

Common Issues

Before reaching out, you may find answers to common questions in our FAQ section.

Account Issues

  • Can't log in? Make sure you're entering the correct email address and check your inbox (including spam) for the one-time passcode (OTP).
  • Want to delete your account? Visit our account deletion page for instructions.

Subscription Issues

  • Billing questions? Subscriptions are managed through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android). Check your store account for billing details.
  • Cancel subscription? Manage your subscription through your device's app store settings.

Response Times

We aim to respond to all support enquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent issues, please call our support line.
